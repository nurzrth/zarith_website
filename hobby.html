My Hobby Of All-Time

1. Listening To Music

Listening to music is one of my favorite hobby because it brings me immense joy, relaxation, and inspiration. It serves as an escape from the stresses of daily life, allowing me to immerse myself in melodies and lyrics that resonate deeply with my emotions and thoughts. Music has a unique ability to lift my mood, whether I need energy to tackle tasks, comfort during challenging times, or motivation to pursue my goals. It also sparks creativity and helps me stay focused while studying or completing assignments. Additionally, exploring different genres and artists broadens my perspective and connects me to diverse cultures and stories. For me, music isn't just entertainment; it’s a companion that enriches my soul, soothes my mind, and helps me express feelings that words often cannot convey.

2. TREASURE EP.3 : One To All

TREASURE EP.3: One To All is the third mini-album by ATEEZ, released on June 10, 2019. The album features a vibrant and summery concept, showcasing the group’s versatility in both sound and style. It includes six tracks, with "Wave" and "Illusion" serving as dual title tracks, each offering a contrasting vibe—"Wave" is uplifting and carefree, while "Illusion" is playful and dreamy. The album explores themes of adventure, youth, and freedom, with tropical beats and energetic melodies that perfectly capture the spirit of summer. This release marked a significant milestone for ATEEZ, earning them their first music show win with "Wave".

3. GOLDEN HOUR : Part.2

GOLDEN HOUR : Part.2 is the second part of the GOLDEN HOUR album series by South Korean singer and ATEEZ member, Hongjoong. The album features a mix of soft and vibrant tracks that showcase his versatile vocal style. It combines elements of pop, R&B, and electronic music, creating a dreamy, mellow atmosphere. The lyrics explore themes of love, self-reflection, and personal growth. The production is clean and polished, with rich instrumentation complementing Hongjoong’s emotive delivery. It offers a captivating listening experience filled with introspective moments and powerful melodies.
